Crypto Beginner Guide

Five important topics to understand cryptocurrency

1. What Is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency that uses cryptography. No middlemen are involved, it's only person to person transactions. Bitcoin was the first crypto, created in 2009. Now there are thousands of cryptocurrencies.

2. How Blockchain Works

A blockchain is a database shared across many devices. Each and every block contains transactions, and once verified, it is added forever. This makes crypto transparent, as anyone can check the data but nobody can change past records.
